01 — Philosophy

Built for the long rally.

I believe business is a lot like volleyball: you don't win on the spike — you win in the thousand small decisions before the ball ever comes to you. Preparation, positioning, and the willingness to dive for something other people would let drop.

I'm a sophomore at USC's Marshall School of Business, a Division I scholarship volleyball player, and the founder of two small businesses. I'm drawn to work where selling, serving people, and building things live in the same room — where the scoreboard is real and the margins are tight.

My goal isn't to be the loudest operator in the room. It's to be the one you want beside you when the game gets close.
